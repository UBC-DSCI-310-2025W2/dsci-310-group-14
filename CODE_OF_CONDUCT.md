# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members of this project pledge to create a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment for everyone involved in the project regardless of background, identity, or level of experience.

## Expected Behaviour

All participants in this project are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Be open to feedback and constructive criticism
- Collaborate openly and support other team members
- Acknowledge the time and effort contributed by others

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following behaviours are not acceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments
- Personal attacks or insulting language
- Publishing private information about others without permission
- Disruptive behaviour that prevents productive collaboration

## Consequences

Participants who engage in unacceptable behaviour may be asked to stop the behaviour, have their contributions removed, or be restricted from participating in the project.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and handled respectfully and confidentially.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct and other open-source community guidelines.
